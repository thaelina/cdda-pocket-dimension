## Pocket Dimension

When starting a new character, that character will be granted a `Dimensional Walk` spell.  
This spell will take 10 minutes to cast.  
If the player is in the default dimension, they will be shifted to the home dimension.  
If the player is in the home dimension, they will be teleported back to the exact spot they left the default dimension.  
The spell will fail in any other dimension.  
This is not a way to escape hordes or any other dangerous situation. The other dimensions do not update when you are not in them, hordes will not wander away, fires will not progress, etc. However time will still pass, if you spend long enough away, you may find newly evolved zombies waiting for you.  

### Notes About Home Dimension
- There is nothing in the home dimension but an endless field that nothing spawns in and Home Central.
- You cannot bring NPCs with you.
- You can shift items on the ground around you *to* the home dimension but not *from* the home dimension.
- If you shift items to the home dimension above or below z-level 0, they will probably not make it there with you.
- You will always appear in the same spot when shifting to the home dimension.
